### Exercise 1.2.4.
 Produce an infinite collection of sets \(A_1, A_2, A_3, \dots\) with the property that every \(A_i\) has an infinite number of elements, \(A_i \cap A_j = \emptyset\) for all \(i \neq j\), and \(\bigcup_{i=1}^{\infty} A_i = \mathbf{N}\).

---
定义 \(p_i\) 表示第 \(i\) 个素数，\(f(x)\) 表示整数 \(x\) 的最小质因数。

则有：
+ \(A_1 = \{n \in \mathbf{Z}|f(x)=p_1\} \cup \{1\}\)
+ \(A_i = \{n \in \mathbf{Z}|f(x)=p_i\}\)
