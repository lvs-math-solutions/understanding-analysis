### Exercise 1.2.2.
 Show that there is no rational number r satisfying \(2^r = 3\).
 
---

 *Proof.* 
假设 \(r\) 为有理数，则存在整数 \(p,q \in \Z,(q\neq0,gcd(p,q)=1)\) 使得

\[ r = \dfrac{p}{q}\]

故 \[ 2^p = 3^q = k\]

故 \(k \mod 2 = 1\) 且 \(k \mod 2 = 0\)
